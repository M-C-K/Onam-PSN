A Tradition That Has Grown With Us

Since 2013, the residents of Prestige Shantiniketan have come together every harvest season to celebrate Onam — not as an event, but as a homecoming. From a humble Pookkalam laid by a handful of families to a three-day extravaganza with live concerts, Theyyam performances, Kalaripayattu and a community Sadya feast, Onam at PSN has become the heartbeat of our togetherness.

Moments That Defined Our Onam Celebrations

Straight from the hearts of our residents, year after year

Agam & Anoop Shankar Experience

"A divine performance" — Agam's rocking finale and the unforgettable Anoop Shankar Experience have brought the house down over the years, ending our festivals with a bang.

Theyyam

Over the years, we have brought eminent artists to perform this breathtaking, fiery ritual art form, giving residents an unforgettable brush with Kerala's mystique.

Kathakali

Renowned Kathakali artists have graced our stage over the years, bringing elaborate makeup, vivid costumes and expressive storytelling to life.

Thayambaka

We have hosted eminent percussionists over the years for this thunderous solo chenda performance, its rhythmic crescendos bringing the audience to its feet.

Kalaripayattu

Centuries-old martial artistry performed live, blending discipline, drama and pure adrenaline.

Stand-up Comedy

Abish Mathew & Sai Kiran had the whole community in splits — "an absolute laugh riot."

Pookkalam

Vibrant floral carpets crafted by residents, turning the courtyard into a canvas of colour.

Onam Sadya

"Lip-smacking" and served with love by legendary caterers like Pazhayidom — the grand feast that always wins hearts.
